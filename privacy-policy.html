Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

At MusicOnTheGo, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our music lesson platform.

Information We Collect

Personal information: Name, email address, profile photo (optional), age group, location and availability.

Musical information: Instruments you teach or learn, skill level, learning goals, practice logs, lesson notes and recordings (with consent).

How We Use Your Information

  • To connect students with music teachers and facilitate lesson bookings
  • To enable communication between teachers and students
  • To track progress and provide feedback
  • To send notifications about lessons and bookings
  • To improve our services

Data Security

We use encrypted data transmission (SSL/TLS), secure password storage, and secure cloud infrastructure. We do not sell your personal information.

Data Sharing

We only share data as needed to facilitate lessons (e.g. with your teacher or student), with trusted service providers who operate our platform, or when required by law.

Your Rights

You can access, update, or delete your data, opt out of marketing, and request a copy of your data. To delete your account, contact us by email.

Children's Privacy

For users under 18, we expect parent/guardian awareness. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13 without parental consent.
