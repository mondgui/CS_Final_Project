Delete Your MusicOnTheGo Account

Request account deletion and data removal

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the MusicOnTheGo app on your device
  2. Go to Settings (tap your profile icon, then Settings)
  3. Scroll to "Account Management" section
  4. Tap "Delete Account"
  5. Confirm deletion by entering your password
  6. Your account will be permanently deleted within 30 days

Alternative Method: Contact Support

If you cannot access the app, you can request account deletion by:

Email us at: support@musiconthego.com

Include in your email:

  • Your registered email address
  • Your full name
  • Subject line: "Account Deletion Request"

We will process your request within 7 business days.

What Data Will Be Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Account Information: Email address, name, password (hashed)
  • Profile Data: Profile picture, bio, location, availability, goals
  • User Content: Practice logs, recordings, notes, messages
  • Booking Information: Lesson bookings, schedules, availability
  • Resources: Uploaded resources, assignments, shared materials
  • Community Posts: All posts and comments you've made

Data Retention Period

After you request account deletion:

  • Your account will be deactivated immediately
  • All data will be permanently deleted within 30 days
  • During this 30-day period, you can contact us to restore your account if you change your mind

Data That May Be Retained

Important: Some data may be retained for legal or operational purposes:

  • Transaction records (if any payments were made) - retained for 7 years for tax/legal compliance
  • Anonymized usage analytics (no personal identifiers)
  • Support ticket history (for customer service records)

What Happens After Deletion

  • You will no longer be able to log in to your account
  • All your content will be removed from the app
  • You will stop receiving notifications and emails
  • You can create a new account with the same email address after 30 days
